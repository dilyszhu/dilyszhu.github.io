hi there!

My name is Dilys and I'm a fellow anxious human.

I started this project because I am fascinated by anxiety. Anxiety can manifest completely differently in each person. I want this site to be a resource from which people can learn more about the disorder and a space where creatives can connect and work together to bring the mental into the physical.

So what does my #anxiety look like?

For me, anxiety is a constant static of negative thoughts running through the back of my mind. To be anxious is to be dragged down and boxed in by irrational fear. It is a group of invisible monsters pulling me back saying, "Wait! That's scary!" Keeping me from being the person I want to be.

Now it's your turn.

What does your anxiety look like? Note that I didn't ask "What does your anxiety feel like?" Try to think outside the box. How does your anxiety manifest in terms of symptoms and behaviors? How would you personify or illustrate your anxiety? Have fun with it.
